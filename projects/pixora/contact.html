Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create collections on Pixora? +

Creating collections is easy! Simply click the "Create Collection" button on your profile page, give it a name and description, and start adding images. You can organize your collections by theme, project, or any category that inspires you.

Can I collaborate with other users on collections? +

Yes! Pixora supports collaborative collections. You can invite other users to contribute to your collections, making it perfect for group projects, team inspiration boards, or shared interests.

How do I report inappropriate content? +

If you come across content that violates our community guidelines, click the three dots (...) on the image or collection and select "Report." Our moderation team will review the content promptly.

Is Pixora available on mobile devices? +

Yes! Pixora is fully responsive and works beautifully on mobile devices. We're also developing dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android to enhance your experience on the go.

How can I become a featured creator on Pixora? +

We're always looking for talented creators to feature! Focus on creating high-quality, original content and engaging with the community. Our curation team regularly scouts for exceptional collections and creators to highlight.
